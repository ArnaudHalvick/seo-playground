# Strategic SEO Pages

This document provides brief descriptions of each page in the Strategic SEO section.

## Landing Page

**Path**: `/strategic-seo/page.tsx`

The main landing page introduces Strategic SEO and organizes all topics into three sections:

1. **Strategy & Planning** - Research and content architecture
2. **Production & Growth** - Content production, measurement, and authority
3. **Tools & Resources** - Tools and templates

Each section contains cards that link to detailed pages. The page includes an overview card explaining what Strategic SEO is and a call-to-action linking to Technical SEO.

## Strategy & Planning

### Research & Strategy

**Path**: `/strategic-seo/research-strategy/`

**Key Topics**:

## Page Structure

All Strategic SEO pages follow a consistent structure:

1. **Header**: Title with icon, description
2. **Philosophy/Overview**: Context-setting card (optional)
3. **Main Content**: Organized sections with cards or lists
4. **Examples/Templates**: Practical examples (where applicable)
5. **Pro Tips**: Best practices and recommendations

## Design Patterns

- **Purple/pink gradient** backgrounds (`from-purple-50 to-slate-50`)
- **Card-based layouts** with hover effects
- **Icons from lucide-react** for visual hierarchy
- **CheckCircle2 icons** for bullet points
- **Alert components** for important notes
- **Gradient cards** for special sections (e.g., starter stacks)
